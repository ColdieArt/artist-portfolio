Decentral Eyes — Elon Musk

An interactive stereoscopic 3D portrait of Elon Musk, built as a kinetic Three.js experience. The portrait can be orbited, rotated, and manipulated in real-time. Coldie's signature technique of layered collage is translated into a fully dimensional digital sculpture.

This artwork includes built-in export functionality — create your own JPEG stills or MP4 recordings directly from the viewer. Each export is a unique iteration shaped by your interaction with the piece.
