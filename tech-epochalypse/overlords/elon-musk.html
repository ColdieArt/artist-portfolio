Overlord I — Elon Musk

Coldie
Three.js / WebGL Interactive

An interactive stereoscopic 3D portrait of Elon Musk, built as a kinetic Three.js experience. The portrait can be orbited, rotated, and manipulated in real-time. Coldie's signature technique of layered collage is translated into a fully dimensional digital sculpture.

This artwork uses stereoscopic 3D rendering to create depth and dimension. The layered composition draws from Coldie's physical collage practice, re-imagined as an interactive WebGL experience. Built-in export functionality lets you capture your own PNG stills or MP4 recordings directly from the viewer.
