Signal / Noise

The Tech Epochalypse is not a prediction -- it is a mirror. Every face in the Decentral Eyes series represents a node in a vast network of power, resistance, creation, and consequence. From Satoshi's ghost to Snowden's refuge, from Buffett's skepticism to Buterin's blueprint -- these portraits map the fault lines of a civilization rebuilding itself on code.

This gallery renders those connections visible. The network is alive. The nodes are watching. The edges carry meaning. Hover over the map to see how surveillance connects to sovereignty, how currency connects to control, how one portrait leads inevitably to the next.

Thematic Connections

The invisible threads that bind the network together.

Thread 01

Surveillance / Privacy

Snowden, Assange, Gensler -- the watchers and the watched. Portraits that interrogate the boundary between transparency and control in the digital age.

Thread 02

Sound Money / Fiat Decay

Buffett, Saylor, the Filthy Fiat series -- the tension between legacy finance and cryptographic truth. Dollar bills decomposing into generative art.

Thread 03

Protocol Architects

Buterin, Dorsey, Antonopoulos -- the builders who turned whitepapers into world-changing networks. Their faces rendered in the very medium they enabled.

Thread 04

Cultural Bridges

Snoop Dogg, Vedder, Bauhaus, Dalai Lama -- where crypto meets culture. These collaborations and portraits prove decentralization extends beyond code.

Thread 05

Machine Provenance

Turing, proof-of-work, Bitcoin ordinals -- the chain of computation from Enigma to the blockchain. Art inscribed in the same ledger as currency.
